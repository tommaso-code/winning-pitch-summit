Why We're Here

What we're trying to achieve over three days.

We're in a strong position — but strong isn't the goal. This is about the next step up.

Three days together to sharpen how every one of us creates value for the families, players, and partners we serve — and to turn that into a pitch that wins. We'll align on where IDA and GSE are headed, level up our sales craft with St. Pierre and Chuck Ervin (PSE), and leave with a shared, sharper story.

  • A stronger value story — more compelling in every prospect conversation.
  • Alignment on 2026-27 targets — one direction across IDA and GSE.
  • Momentum & relationships — leave energized and connected for the season ahead.
